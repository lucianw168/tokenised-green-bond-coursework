# Word Budget

> Hard limit: **2,400 words** (excluding references, appendix, and figure captions)

## Budget Allocation

| Section | Min | Target | Max | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Introduction | 100 | 130 | 150 | Problem + asset + argument only. No feature lists. |
| 1. Asset Description and Value Source | 330 | 370 | 400 | Bond first, green bond second, token third. |
| 2. Rationale for Tokenisation | 280 | 320 | 350 | At least 4 benefits. Must caveat liquidity. |
| 3. Token Structure and Design | 380 | 420 | 450 | **Core section.** FT rationale + rights + controls + lifecycle. Includes implementation note. |
| 4. Market Access and Liquidity Design | 330 | 370 | 400 | Full lifecycle. Do NOT overstate liquidity. |
| 5. Risk Analysis and Limitations | 380 | 420 | 450 | **Highest scoring.** 5 risk categories, each specific. |
| 6. Role of DeFi and Blockchain Infrastructure | 180 | 220 | 250 | Restrained. Programmable infrastructure framing. |
| Conclusion | 80 | 110 | 120 | Judgment only. No summary. |
| **Total** | **2,060** | **2,360** | **2,570** | Edit down if over 2,400. |

## Priority Ranking

If you need to cut words, cut from these sections first (lowest impact on marks):

1. Introduction (can be tightened to 100 words)
2. Section 6 / DeFi (can be tightened to 180 words)
3. Conclusion (can be tightened to 80 words)

Never cut from these sections (highest impact on marks):

1. Section 5 / Risk — at least 400 words
2. Section 3 / Token Design — at least 380 words
3. Section 4 / Market Access — at least 330 words

## What Counts Towards Word Limit

- All body text in Sections 1–6, Introduction, and Conclusion
- Implementation note text (but keep it minimal — ~40 words)
- In-text figure references

## What Does NOT Count

- Reference list
- Appendix (screenshot table, evidence matrix pointer)
- Figure captions
- Title
